A cute robot companion, three takes

A minimalist, kawaii robot tutor. The orb becomes the robot's face: friendly eyes, a tiny smile, soft "listening" rings when it hears you. Clean and simple, not busy or cheap. All bright, light, English-only. Same voice + transcript + report flow underneath.

How to choose: pick A, B, or C (or mix, e.g. "B's face, C's lavender"). The face also has Connecting, Listening, and Speaking expressions, I will animate those when I build the real one. Then I wire it into page.py and strip all Chinese from the UI.
